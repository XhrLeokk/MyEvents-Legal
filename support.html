订阅购买由 Apple App Store 处理。开发者不会直接接收或保存银行卡信息、Apple ID 密码或支付账号信息。

  • A screenshot or short steps to reproduce the issue;
  • For subscription issues, mention whether you have tried Restore Purchases.

Subscriptions and Restore Purchases

Subscription purchases are handled by Apple App Store. The developer does not directly receive or store payment card information, Apple ID passwords, or payment account information.

If you have subscribed but Plus features are not unlocked, tap Restore Purchases in the app first. If access is still not restored, confirm that the device is signed in with the Apple ID used for the subscription.

Refunds

App Store payments and refunds are handled by Apple. The developer cannot directly process refund requests. You can request a refund through Apple's official refund page.

https://reportaproblem.apple.com/
