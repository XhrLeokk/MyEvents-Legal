一个面向 CS 赛事复盘的 iPad 数据分析 App，用赛程、比赛表格、选手数据、热力图与 Radar 分析整理近年大赛表现。

An iPad-first CS esports analysis app for reviewing tournaments through schedules, match tables, player data, heatmaps, and Radar views.

数据分析

About the App

My CS Events is an independent esports event analysis app. It is designed for viewers who want to review CS tournaments after they finish, compare players and teams, and understand performance patterns beyond the final score.

The app does not provide messaging, user-generated content, gambling, contests, or unrestricted web browsing.

Independent Reference

This app does not use third-party player photos, team photos, or logos. It is an independent analytical reference based on publicly available factual event data and is not affiliated with tournament organizers, teams, players, game publishers, or HLTV.
