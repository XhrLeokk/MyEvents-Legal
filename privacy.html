隐私政策

App：我的赛事 / My Events
生效日期：2026 年 5 月 20 日

本隐私政策说明“我的赛事 / My Events”（以下简称“本 App”）如何处理用户信息。本 App 由个人开发者提供。

1. 我们不创建账号

本 App 第一版不提供账号系统，也不要求用户注册、登录或提交姓名、邮箱、电话号码等身份信息。

2. 我们不主动收集个人信息

本 App 不主动收集以下信息：

  • 姓名、邮箱、电话号码或通讯录；
  • 精确位置信息；
  • 广告标识符；
  • 用户生成内容；
  • 支付卡号或账单地址。

3. 订阅与购买

本 App 的订阅购买由 Apple App Store 和 StoreKit 处理。开发者不会直接接收或保存用户的银行卡信息、支付账号或 Apple ID 密码。

本 App 会在设备本地通过 StoreKit 判断“我的赛事+ / My Events+”订阅是否有效，以决定是否解锁对应功能。第一版不将订阅状态上传到开发者服务器。

4. 本地数据

本 App 可能在设备本地保存应用设置、订阅状态缓存、数据版本状态等运行所需信息。这些信息用于改善本机使用体验，不会在第一版中上传到开发者服务器。

5. 第三方服务

本 App 第一版不接入第三方广告 SDK、第三方分析 SDK 或第三方崩溃统计 SDK。

6. 儿童隐私

本 App 不面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会在知情情况下收集儿童个人信息。

7. 政策更新

如果未来本 App 增加账号系统、远程数据更新、统计分析、崩溃日志或其他可能涉及数据处理的功能，我们会更新本隐私政策，并在适当位置提示用户。

如对本隐私政策有任何问题，请通过以下邮箱联系开发者：

Privacy Policy

App: My Events
Effective Date: May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how My Events handles user information. The app is provided by an individual developer.

1. No Account System

The first version of the app does not provide an account system and does not require users to register, sign in, or submit identity information such as name, email address, or phone number.

2. No Active Collection of Personal Information

The app does not actively collect the following information:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or contacts;
  • Precise location data;
  • Advertising identifiers;
  • User-generated content;
  • Payment card numbers or billing addresses.

3. Subscriptions and Purchases

Subscription purchases are handled by Apple App Store and StoreKit. The developer does not directly receive or store payment card information, payment account information, or Apple ID passwords.

The app uses StoreKit on the device to determine whether the My Events+ subscription is active and to unlock the corresponding features. In the first version, the app does not upload subscription status to a developer server.

4. Local Data

The app may store app settings, subscription status cache, data version state, and other necessary runtime information locally on the device. This information is used for the on-device app experience and is not uploaded to a developer server in the first version.

5. Third-Party Services

The first version of the app does not integrate third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or third-party crash reporting SDKs.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If the app later adds account features, remote data updates, analytics, crash reporting, or other features that may involve data processing, this Privacy Policy will be updated and users will be notified where appropriate.
